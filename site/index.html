Al-Adwan Company was established in the
mid-nineties of the last century, with the
increasing need for companies specialized
in developing growing real estate projects.

In 1994, Al-Adwan Real Estate Development was established with a commitment to excellence in residential, commercial, and mixed-use properties across Saudi Arabia. Our journey began with a vision to shape urban landscapes and enhance the quality of life for residents through innovative and meticulously planned projects.
Al-Adwan’s approach is rooted in human-centered design, adapting our projects to meet the evolving needs of communities. We provide integrated real estate solutions that address contemporary challenges, ensuring our developments have a positive social and environmental impact.

As we look to the future, our goal is to expand our expertise and services to neighboring countries, reinforcing our leadership in real estate development. Aligning with Saudi Vision 2030, through strategic planning and a focus on sustainable growth, Al-Adwan continues to set new benchmarks in the industry, contributing to the economic and social development of the regions we serve.
